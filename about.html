About Me

At Palm Studios we offer a variety of services from Hair care, nail care, waxing, beauty supplies and boutique. Education and a drive to always be learning are a cornerstone of who we are. We are artists who believe in using the best quality products. Sanitation and cleanliness are of the upmost importance. We strive to create a beautiful, comfortable environment for you to relax and enjoy receiving services. Shelley is a Redken Specialist and a Certified Painted Hair Artist who has trained in NYC, San Francisco, San Diego, LA, Las Vegas and all over the USA. Book an appointment to come soak your feet in our beautiful Egyptian Copper Tub. Be waxed with our lovely and gentle French wax. Or let us help you love your hair. We will help you have all the tools and product you need to achieve your look at home.
